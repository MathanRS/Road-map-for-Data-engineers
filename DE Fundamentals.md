# Module 02: Core Concepts Every DE Must Know (14 Lessons)

### 13. Batch Processing vs Stream Processing — When to Use What *(Free)*
Understand the fundamental difference between batch and stream processing and learn when each approach is the right choice.

### 14. ETL vs ELT — The Modern Debate *(Free)*
Compare ETL and ELT approaches, understand why ELT has gained popularity, and know when each pattern fits best.

### 15. Data Warehouse vs Data Lake vs Lakehouse — Explained Simply *(Free)*
A plain-language comparison of three major data storage architectures and guidance on when to use each one.

### 16. OLTP vs OLAP — Why It Matters for Your Pipeline
Learn how transactional and analytical databases differ and why choosing the wrong one can break your pipeline.

### 17. Data Serialization Formats: Parquet, Avro, ORC, JSON, CSV
A practical comparison of the most common data formats—when to use columnar vs row-based formats and why it matters for performance.

### 18. Data Modeling for Data Engineers: Star Schema, Snowflake Schema & Dimensional Modeling
Master the foundational data modeling patterns every DE must know—fact tables, dimension tables, star vs snowflake schemas, and slowly changing dimensions.

### 19. Pipeline Design Patterns: Incremental Loads, Full Refresh, Upserts & SCD
The four loading strategies every DE must know—full refresh, incremental append, upsert/merge, and SCD—with SQL examples and a decision framework.

### 20. Data Quality: What It Means and How to Measure It
Define data quality dimensions—accuracy, completeness, freshness, consistency—and learn practical ways to measure and monitor them.

### 21. Data Lineage & Pipeline Observability: Know When Your Data Breaks
Learn how to trace where your data came from, detect when pipelines fail silently, and build observability into your data platform from day one.

### 22. Data Governance & Compliance Basics (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA)
An introduction to data governance frameworks and key regulatory requirements every data engineer should understand.

### 23. Idempotency — The Most Important Concept in Data Pipelines
Why idempotent pipelines are essential for reliability, and how to design operations that produce the same result no matter how many times they run.

### 24. Schema Evolution & Backward/Forward Compatibility
How to evolve your data schemas safely without breaking downstream consumers—covering backward, forward, and full compatibility.

### 25. Partitioning & Clustering: How to Make Warehouse Queries 10× Faster
Learn how partitioning and clustering reduce query costs and latency in cloud warehouses—with concrete examples in BigQuery and Snowflake.

### 26. Exactly-Once vs At-Least-Once vs At-Most-Once Delivery
Understand the three delivery guarantees in distributed systems and their trade-offs for data pipeline design.
